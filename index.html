The Oxygen Advantage is a book with a singular claim: breath right, and every cell, muscle and organ will level up. A lifetime of breathing through the mouth, modern diets, and activities sedentary has left the respiratory system confused. Instead of ensuring a hearty supply of oxygen to my cells, the more intensely I breath the more I'm actually suffocating my organs!

You can measure it, claims the author, with a Body Oxygen Level Test (BOLT). A simple breath holding exercise that exposes the character of your respiratory system.
